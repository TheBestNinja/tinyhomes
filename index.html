What We Build

We build custom tiny homes on wheels. Whether you have a design in mind or need inspiration, we can help you realize it. Our clients have requested starter homes, lake cabins for two, or a safe place for a loved one to stay close. We're open to hearing any request.

We specialize in Tiny Homes on Wheels, which offer several advantages:

  • Regulations: A home on wheels can bypass certain home regulations that a foundation-built structure cannot.
  • Mobility: Your choice of location doesn't have to be permanent—you can take it with you when you move.
  • Resale Value: Tiny homes can be resold when you're done with them, much more conveniently than an on-site built structure.
